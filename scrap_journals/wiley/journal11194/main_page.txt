			<tr><td colspan="2" class="field">Description:</td></tr>
	<tr><td colspan="2">Most medical communities are eager to learn how to help their patients with stroke, which by the year 2025 will be the greatest disabler of people worldwide. The International Journal of Stroke is the new journal of the International Stroke Society. Blackwell Publishing are pleased to announce the partnership of a new journal with the International Stroke Society. The International Journal of Stroke offers pharmaceutical companies a new platform with which to reach international audiences. As the only true international journal in the field of stroke, as reflected in the board and readership of the journal, you will reach professionals and clinicians who are dedicated to reducing the burden of stroke worldwide. The International Journal of Stroke is a welcome addition to the international stroke journal landscape in that it will emphasise the broader aspects of stroke which should be of interest to all those professionals working toward reducing the burden of stroke worldwide. Hence, there will be items of news interest about stroke from all parts of the world so that each region of the world becomes a stakeholder.</td></tr>
