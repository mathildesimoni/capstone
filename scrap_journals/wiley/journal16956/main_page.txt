	<tr><td colspan="2">The aim of the journal Mass Spectrometry Reviews is to publish well-written reviews in selected topics in the various sub-fields of mass spectrometry as a means to summarize the research that has been performed in that area to focus attention of other researchers to critically review the published material and to stimulate further research in that area. The scope of the published reviews include but are not limited to topics such as theoretical treatments instrumental design ionization methods analyzers detectors application to the qualitative and quantitative analysis of various compounds or elements basic ion chemistry and structure studies ion energetic studies and studies on biomolecules polymers etc.</td></tr>
