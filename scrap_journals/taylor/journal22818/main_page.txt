The journal encourages work that is innovative in both perspective and formal approach, that goes beyond conventional cultural politics and cultural studies, and that suggests new approaches to both culture and politics. The journal believes that theory does not mean simply the application of existing high theory to examples of culture and politics, but new ways of theorizing culture and politics. Strategies: Journal of Theory, Culture &amp; Politics therefore welcomes essays from all cultural perspectives, and especially encourages the inclusion of non-European, womens, queer, multi-cultural and other perspectives.
