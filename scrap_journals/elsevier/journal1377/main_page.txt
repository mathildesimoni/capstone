	<tr><td colspan="2">Annales mdico-psychologiques aims at reporting the major trends in current psychiatry and publishing clinical and biological research works at the international scientific level. The first French journal of psychiatry (founded in 1843), it is the official journal of the Socit mdico-psychologique. Annales mdico-psychologiques publishes original reports in the domain of psychiatry and psychopathology and also communications presented at sessions of the Socit mdico-psychologique, reviews of books, literature, videos, etc. , reports of psychiatric societies meetings, colloquiums, symposiums and information relevant to the discipline.</td></tr>
