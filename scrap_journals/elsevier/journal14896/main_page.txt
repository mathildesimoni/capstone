	<tr><td colspan="2">Each issue of the Journal of Physiology (Paris) is specially commissioned, and provides an overview of one important area of neuroscience research, by publishing both review and research papers from the leading researchers in that field. All areas of the neurosciences are represented in the journals coverage. Content will be of interest to not only researchers specialized in the study of brain and nervous system, but also to those working in interdisciplinary disciplines of the life sciences, including cognitive and computational neurosciences. The journal does not accept spontaneously submitted research papers. The Editor-in Chief does though welcome suggestions for topics to be covered, and encourages preselection of specialized symposia proceedings 6 to 12 months before their occurrence.</td></tr>
