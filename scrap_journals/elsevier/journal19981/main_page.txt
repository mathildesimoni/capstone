			<tr><td colspan="2" class="field">Description:</td></tr>
	<tr><td colspan="2">This journal provides the most current information on primary and preventive health care for women. Published in cooperation with the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists (ACOG), PRIMARY CARE UPDATE FOR OB/GYNS is published in six issues per year. PRIMARY CARE UPDATE FOR OB/GYNS presents practical clinical information pertaining to the diagnosis, treatment, and management of non-ob/gyn disorders in women. Each issue contains peer-reviewed articles on: cancer, cardiovascular disease, infectious disease, psychiatry and preventive health care. Other regular topics featured in the journal cover such areas as gastroenterology, pulmonology, pain, endocrinology, neurology, and dermatology, to fully encompass the health care needs of female patients. PRIMARY CARE UPDATE FOR OB/GYNS covers important health care concerns including: coronary artery disease in women; hypertension; anemia; melanoma; colon cancer; hepatitis; domestic violence; substance abuse; smoking cessation; chronic fatigue syndrome. The journal is an essential resource for ob/gyns and other health care providers who wish to enhance the scope of services that they provide for their patients.</td></tr>
