In addition to full-length articles, brief notes as well as position papers are welcome. This international journal also provides Forums for the exchange of ideas and opinions and a column entitles Times and Trends. The overriding criteria for publication are originality, high scientific quality and up-to-date relevance. Papers should be written with a wide audience of microbiologists in mind.</td></tr>
