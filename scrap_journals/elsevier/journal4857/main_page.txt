			<tr><td colspan="2" class="field">Description:</td></tr>
	<tr><td colspan="2">The series IIa of the Comptes Rendus de lAcadmie des Sciences covers all fields of earth and planetary sciences as well as the environment, presented in the following categories: External Geophysics, Climate, and Environment / Internal Geophysics / Geomaterials /Geochemistry / Surface Geoscience / Oceanography / Stratigraphy / Tectonics / Geodynamics / Paleontology / Prehistory / Planets / History of Sciences. The articles presented are original notes describing briefly an important discovery, review papers and articles in history of sciences. Each manuscript is submitted to an expert in the field. The series devoted to Earth and Planetary Sciences is published bi-monthly, i. e. 24 issues a year, which leads to rapid publication of significant findings from scientists. The articles are usually published within one or two months after the manuscript has been accepted. Articles are published in English or in French and at least one page abridged version in the other language is systematically offered. Circulation is expanding worldwide with an increasing number of readers and authors, particularly in countries where scientific progress is significant.</td></tr>
