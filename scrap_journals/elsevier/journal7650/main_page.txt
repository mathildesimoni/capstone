Published monthly, each issue starts with a brief editorial and then provides a series of concise summaries on business, market and technical developments from a global perspective. The newsletter covers: market trends, company news, including information on new plants worldwide, technological innovations, environmental issues and applications, new patents, conferences and book reviews.</td></tr>
