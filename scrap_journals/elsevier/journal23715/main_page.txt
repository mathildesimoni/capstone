	<tr><td colspan="2">The Review will publish four issues per year, beginning with calendar year 1998. The Review provides a forum where practitioners and researchers in industry, academia, and government throughout the world can find research results, new ideas, new applications of knowledge, and discussions of important topics that can help them enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of the worldwide food and agribusiness system. Since our audience encompasses the broad spectrum of food industry leaders worldwide. The Review emphasizes articles and decision cases that deal with relevant issues confronting the food system, are applications oriented, and are clearly written. Authors who use quantitative techniques should use them as a means to accomplish their objectives, rather than as the central focus of their manuscript. The International Food and Agribusiness Management Association is an association of food and agribusiness industry leaders from around the world and managers of public, private, and cooperative commercial organizations; educators; scientists; policy makers; and others concerned that the value-added food and fiber chains should be economically efficient, responsive to nutritional and other human needs, global, and sustainable.
