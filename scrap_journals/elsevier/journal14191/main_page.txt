	<tr><td colspan="2">The Journal of Magnetic Resonance presents original papers in the fields of nuclear magnetic resonance, electron spin resonance, and nuclear quadrupole resonance spectroscopy, as well as in related experimental techniques. The subjects covered include the principles underlying these methods; significant experimental results, especially those leading to spectral correlations likely to be of general interest to scientists in these areas; and descriptions of apparatus, experimental techniques, procedures of spectral analysis and interpretation, and computational methods. Discontinued - now Journal of Magnetic Resonance A and B.</td></tr>
