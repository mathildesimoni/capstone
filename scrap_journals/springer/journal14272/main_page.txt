	<tr><td colspan="2">Journal of Materials Synthesis and Processing is an invaluable resource for the publication of theoretical modeling studies and experimental research on all aspects of the synthesis and processing of materials. The journal offers thorough coverage of a broad range of topics including ceramic intermetallic and composite substances. A distinguished editorial board of internationally renowned scientists ensures that the peer-reviewed original papers and review articles are timely and of the highest quality. Featured topics include: combustion synthesis of superconducting oxides; chemical synthesis and processing of nanophase materials; liquid-phase reaction-bonded structural ceramics and composites; centrifugal casting of ceramic components; explosive shock synthesis and compaction of structural ceramics and composites; and plasma synthesis of superconducting oxides among others.</td></tr>
