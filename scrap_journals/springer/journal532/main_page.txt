			<tr><td colspan="2" class="field">Description:</td></tr>
	<tr><td colspan="2">Discontinued in 1999. Reproductive research aimed at both improving our understanding of the biological systems that govern our fertility and the development of methods of fertility regulation is undergoing a significant technological advances that will have an important impact on human fertility and social behavior. Advances in Contraception by reporting international efforts in basic reproductive research and clinical aspects of contraception represents a major forum for publication in this rapidly expanding field. The journal is the official journal of the Society for the Advancement of Contraception (SAC) and publishes original peer reviewed papers concerned with reproductive research and clinical aspects of contraception as well as review papers. Advances in Contraception is essential reading for all those involved in this vitally important field. Free supplements on highly topical subjects such as oral contraceptives cardiovascular disease and enhancing oral contraceptive compliance and efficiency are published regularly.</td></tr>
