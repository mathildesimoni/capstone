	<tr><td colspan="2">Published quarterly, Frontiers of History in China - Selected Publications from Chinese Universities is to present the best research achievements by professors and students of Chinese Universities on various sub-fields of History. It is intended to facilitate effective communication and exchanges between historians in China and abroad. Current history research in China incorporates the modern methods of history writing originated from the West and Chinese value of historical perspectives. Historians in China demonstrate their unique insights with regards to many historical events, not only in Chinese history, but also in world history, which should be a great contribution to the related scholarship in the international academic community. Covering topics such as economic development, commercialization, urbanization, intellectual movement, cultural exchanges between China and the West, gender and family relations, etc. , the journal is addressed to college students, professors and researchers, as well as ordinary readers interested in Chinese culture.</td></tr>
